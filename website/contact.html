FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our HealthPlix portal, call us directly at +91 98765 43210, or visit the hospital in person. Online booking is available 24/7 for your convenience.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card and policy details during your visit. Our billing department can help verify your coverage.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Please bring a valid ID proof, insurance card (if applicable), any previous medical records or test results, list of current medications, and your referral letter if you have one.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent ENT conditions. For emergencies, please call our emergency number or visit the hospital immediately.

How long does a typical consultation take?

A typical consultation takes 20-30 minutes. However, the duration may vary depending on the complexity of your condition and any diagnostic tests required.
