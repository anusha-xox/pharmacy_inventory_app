Ear Care Services

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for all ear-related conditions

Conditions We Treat

  • Hearing Loss: Comprehensive evaluation and treatment
  • Ear Infections: Acute and chronic otitis media treatment
  • Tinnitus: Management of ringing in ears
  • Vertigo & Balance Disorders: Diagnosis and treatment
  • Ear Wax Removal: Safe and effective removal
  • Perforated Eardrum: Treatment and surgical repair

Procedures & Treatments

  • Audiometry and hearing tests
  • Tympanometry
  • Ear microscopy
  • Myringotomy and grommet insertion
  • Tympanoplasty (eardrum repair)
  • Hearing aid consultation

Nose Care Services

Advanced treatment for nasal and sinus conditions

Conditions We Treat

  • Sinusitis: Acute and chronic sinus infections
  • Nasal Polyps: Medical and surgical management
  • Deviated Septum: Correction for improved breathing
  • Allergic Rhinitis: Comprehensive allergy management
  • Nasal Obstruction: Treatment for breathing difficulties
  • Nosebleeds: Diagnosis and treatment

Procedures & Treatments

  • Nasal endoscopy
  • Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery (FESS)
  • Septoplasty
  • Turbinate reduction
  • Polypectomy
  • Allergy testing

Throat Care Services

Expert care for throat, voice, and swallowing disorders

Conditions We Treat

  • Tonsillitis: Acute and chronic tonsil infections
  • Pharyngitis: Throat infections and inflammation
  • Voice Disorders: Hoarseness and vocal cord problems
  • Swallowing Difficulties: Dysphagia evaluation
  • Sleep Apnea: Diagnosis and treatment options
  • Laryngitis: Voice box inflammation treatment

Procedures & Treatments

  • Laryngoscopy (flexible and rigid)
  • Tonsillectomy and adenoidectomy
  • Microlaryngeal surgery
  • Vocal cord surgery
  • Sleep apnea surgery
  • Voice therapy
