Otology Department

Specialized Ear Care and Treatment

Conditions Treated

  • Hearing Loss: Comprehensive evaluation and management
  • Ear Infections: Acute and chronic otitis media treatment
  • Tinnitus: Ringing in ears diagnosis and therapy
  • Balance Disorders: Vertigo and dizziness management
  • Ear Drum Perforation: Surgical and non-surgical repair

Services Offered

  • Pure tone audiometry
  • Tympanometry
  • Hearing aid fitting and counseling
  • Ear microsurgery
  • Vestibular function testing

Rhinology Department

Expert Nasal and Sinus Care

Conditions Treated

  • Sinusitis: Acute and chronic sinus infections
  • Nasal Polyps: Medical and surgical management
  • Deviated Septum: Septoplasty procedures
  • Allergic Rhinitis: Allergy testing and treatment
  • Nasal Obstruction: Breathing difficulty solutions

Services Offered

  • Nasal endoscopy
  • CT scan interpretation
  • Functional endoscopic sinus surgery (FESS)
  • Septoplasty and turbinoplasty
  • Allergy immunotherapy

Laryngology Department

Voice and Throat Disorder Specialists

Conditions Treated

  • Voice Disorders: Hoarseness and vocal cord problems
  • Throat Infections: Tonsillitis and pharyngitis
  • Swallowing Disorders: Dysphagia management
  • Laryngeal Tumors: Benign and malignant lesions
  • Sleep Apnea: Snoring and breathing disorders

Services Offered

  • Laryngoscopy and videostroboscopy
  • Voice therapy and rehabilitation
  • Microlaryngeal surgery
  • Tonsillectomy and adenoidectomy
  • Sleep study and CPAP therapy
